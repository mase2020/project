Syllabus

Our syllabus.

Our current syllabus comprises of a mixture of the Safar Academy curriculum as well as material prepared by our own institution. The syllabus covers a range of topics which will strengthen your child's fundamental Islamic knowledge and in sha Allah make them a beacon of Islam. Topics range from Aqeedah, Seerah, Fiqh, Salaah, Duas, Arabic, Qaaidah and Quran with Tajweed.

Qaaidah

Children will start by identifying Arabic letters with the correct pronunciations. By the end of the Qaaidah, children should be able to read full sentences in the Arabic language including excerpts from the Quran.

Quran with Tajweed

Children will concentrate on becoming fluent in reciting the Quran with the correct tajweed. When a child is confident and is able to recite from anywhere in the Quran with the correct rules of tajweed and with good fluency, only then will they be able to proceed to hifzul-Quran(memorisation of the Quran).

Hifz

Children will memorise portions of the Quran starting from Juz 30. Hifz lessons will be divided into three portions; 1) New memorisation. 2) Current juz revision. 3) Revision. The aim is to create Huffaz of the Quran who will become ambassadors of the religion of Islam.

Arabic Language

Children will learn the arabic language with qualified arabic speaking teachers. They will focus on the Easy Arabic Course from the Islamic University of Madina.

Duas/Surahs

Children will learn their essential daily Duas. The syllabus ranges from daily duas, duas of salah, janazah, gaining closenes to Allah, dhikr etc. Children on Quran level will be memorising surahs alongside their Duas.

Islamic Studies

This will cover various topics such as Fiqh, Aqeedah, Stories of the Prophets and Islamic history. Children will be shown practical demonstrations of Salaah and other important aspects of Islam.

About Us

Our Project

Madina Jame Masjid

Madina Jame Masjid is the first masjid to be established in the Isle of Dogs community in the year of 1991. The masjid serves a wide range of services from daily salah, children's madrasa, adult classes and it can cater for nikah. The masjid also serves the youth and elders alike with weekly lectures and programs.

Weekend Madrasa

Our weekend madrasa expanded to Madina Jame Masjid due to the influx of Muslim residents moving in. The madrasa has helped cater for our local children as well as children from other regions.
